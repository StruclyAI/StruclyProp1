01Getting Started

Open StruclyProp and
accept the disclaimer.

StruclyProp runs entirely in your browser. No installation, no account, no download needed. Open the link, accept the legal terms, and start analysing immediately.

1
Open the link
Go to callumskunk.github.io/StruclyProp in Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge. Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
2
Read and accept the disclaimer
A legal disclaimer modal appears on first load. Read it carefully — StruclyProp is not financial advice and is not FSCA registered. Tick the checkbox and click I UNDERSTAND — OPEN STRUCLYPROP.
3
Enter your Anthropic API key (for AI Verdict only)
The gold banner asks for an API key. This is only needed for the AI Analyst Summary. All financial calculations work without it. Get a free key at console.anthropic.com — paste it in and click SAVE KEY. The key is stored only in your browser.
4
Start entering your deal
All inputs start at zero. Every field you change updates the results in real time — there is no submit button. Start with Purchase Price and Gross Monthly Rent and everything else flows from there.
💡 Tip
Save your deal before closing the browser — click the 💾 Save button at the top of the input panel, give it a name, and it will be available in the Load dropdown next time you open StruclyProp.

03Acquisition Inputs

Acquisition &
purchase costs.

Start here. Transfer duty is auto-calculated from the SARS 2024/25 scale the moment you enter the purchase price.

FieldDefaultWhat to enter
Purchase PriceR 0The agreed purchase price. Transfer duty calculates automatically.
Zero on new builds from a VAT-registered developer.
Deposit %0%Your cash deposit as % of purchase price. SA banks require minimum 10% for investment properties.
Higher deposit = better LTV = often better interest rate.
Transfer Attorney FeesR 0Conveyancing fees — typically R15k–R40k depending on purchase price.

04Bond & Financing

Bond terms &
interest rate.

FieldDefaultWhat to enter
Annual Interest Rate0%SA Prime Rate + your bank's spread. Prime is 11.25% (May 2025). Most investors are quoted Prime + 1–2%. Enter the full nominal annual rate.
Get a pre-qualification from a bond originator for your exact rate.
Loan Term0 yearsRepayment period. SA standard is 20 years. A shorter term reduces total interest but raises monthly repayments.
⚠ Rate Sensitivity
A 1% change in interest rate on a R1.35M bond changes monthly repayments by roughly R700–R800/month. Always run the Stress Test in the Cash Flow tab.
05Rental Income

Rental income
assumptions.

Be conservative. Using optimistic rent and vacancy assumptions is the single most common mistake first-time landlords make.

FieldDefaultWhat to enter
Gross Monthly RentR 0Achievable market rent at 100% occupancy. Check PayProp, Property24, or TPN for comparables.
Use the middle of the range — not the top. Top rent requires perfect property and perfect tenant.
Vacancy Rate0%Expected proportion of time the unit is vacant. SA residential norm is 5–10%.
StruclyProp uses this to calculate Effective Gross Income = Gross Rent × (1 − Vacancy).
Annual Rental Escalation0%Expected annual rent increase. Typically CPI-linked at 6–8%. This drives the 5-year and 10-year projections.

leaves your pocket.

FieldDefaultWhat to enter
Rates & TaxesR 0/moMonthly municipal rates. Verify with the municipality — varies by area and GV valuation.
LeviesR 0/moBody corporate / HOA levy for sectional title or estate. Zero for freehold standalone properties.
Building InsuranceR 0/moBuilding insurance is the landlord's responsibility. Contents is the tenant's. Budget R400–R1,200/month.
Management Fee %0%Percentage of effective rent paid to the managing agent. SA market rate is 8–12% + VAT.
Maintenance Reserve %0% p.a.Annual maintenance as % of property value. Rule of thumb: 1% newer, 1.5–2% older stock.
UtilitiesR 0/moOnly if you pay electricity/water directly. Zero if tenants have separate accounts.
Accounting & LegalR 0/yrAnnual tax return, bookkeeping, and legal costs. Budget R2,500–R6,000 p.a.
Special LeviesR 0/yrBody corporate special levies for capital projects. Enter expected annual average.
07Growth & Tax

Capital growth
& your tax rate.

FieldDefaultWhat to enter
Annual Capital Growth0%Expected annual property appreciation. SA long-run nominal average is 6–8%. Use FNB HPI or Lightstone data for suburb-specific estimates.
Be conservative. Real (inflation-adjusted) growth has been closer to 0–2% recently.
Marginal Income Tax Rate0%Your SARS marginal rate. Rental income adds to your total taxable income. Valid brackets: 18, 26, 31, 36, 39, 41, 45%.
Bond interest and all operating expenses are deductible — StruclyProp calculates your monthly tax shield.
08Advanced Modelling

IRR, exit &
stress testing.

FieldDefaultWhat to enter
Hold Period0 yrsHow long you plan to hold before selling. Drives IRR, NPV, and exit valuation. Typically 5–10 years.
Hurdle Rate0%Your minimum acceptable annualised return. SA investors typically use 12–15%. If IRR > hurdle, the deal clears your bar.
Exit Cap Rate0%The yield the market will apply to your NOI at exit to price the property. Be conservative — use 7–9% for residential.
Exit Agent Commission0%Selling agent fee. SA norm is 5–7.5% + VAT.
CGT Inclusion Rate0%Individual = 40% · Trust = 80% · Company = 80% (at lower corporate rate). The Advanced tab compares all three.
Number of Units05+ units qualifies for Section 13sex — 5% p.a. depreciation on building cost. Powerful portfolio tax break.
Stress Test (bps)0Rate hike scenario. 200bps = Prime +2%. Shows how resilient cash flow is to SARB rate increases.
Expense Inflation0%Annual escalation of operating expenses in the 10-year DCF model. Typically 5–7%.
DCF Exit Year0Year of assumed sale in the DCF model. Exit value = final year NOI ÷ exit cap rate.
09Capital Stack

Deal structuring &
waterfall logic.

FieldDefaultWhat to enter
GP Equity Split0%General Partner equity as % of total equity. LP contributes the remainder. GP typically 10–30%.
LP Preferred Return0%LPs receive this annual return before the GP earns any promote. Industry standard 6–8% p.a., compounded.
GP Promote0%GP's share of profits above the preferred return hurdle. 20% promote = GP earns 20c per rand above hurdle.
10Overview Tab

📊 Overview —
the deal at a glance.

Default view when results open. Covers acquisition summary, bond repayment, yield analysis, benchmarks, and 5-year projection.

Key metrics in this tab

Gross Yield
Gross Annual Rent ÷ Purchase Price
Pre-expense yield. First filter — below 7% in SA, scrutinise carefully.
SA target: ≥7%
Net Yield
NOI ÷ Purchase Price
The definitive yield. If net yield > interest rate, leverage creates positive carry.
SA target: 4–6%
Total Equity Deployed
Deposit + All Acquisition Costs
Real day-1 cash outlay — the denominator for ROE. Not just the deposit.
5-Year Growth Table
Year-by-year amortisation
Property value, bond balance, equity, cumulative CF, and ROE for each year.
11Cash Flow Tab

💰 Cash Flow —
what actually hits your account.

Pre-Tax Monthly CF
Eff. Rent − Expenses − Bond
Negative = monthly top-up from salary. Positive = property pays you.
After-Tax Monthly CF
Pre-Tax CF + Tax Shield
True economic position. Bond interest and expenses are SARS deductible.
DSCR
NOI ÷ Annual Debt Service
SA banks require ≥1.2×. Below 1.0× = bond will likely be declined.
Target: ≥1.2×
Danger: below 1.0×
Break-Even Vacancy
1 − (Expenses + Bond) ÷ Gross Rent
Maximum vacancy before cash flow turns negative. Higher = more resilient.
Target: >15%

Sensitivity Matrix

A 3×3 colour-coded grid: monthly cash flow across combinations of ±2% rate change and ±3% vacancy change. Shows exactly where the deal turns red.

Stress Test

Recalculates repayment and cash flow at your current rate plus your chosen basis points. If stressed CF is still manageable, the deal survives a rate cycle.

12Advanced Tab

📈 Advanced —
IRR, exit & tax structure.

IRR (After-Tax)
Newton-Raphson on annual CFs + exit
Annualised return on equity over your hold period. If IRR > hurdle rate, the deal creates value.
NPV at Hurdle Rate
Σ (CF_t ÷ (1+hurdle)^t)
Positive = deal adds value above minimum return. Negative = doesn't clear your bar.
Equity Multiple (MoIC)
Total Returns ÷ Equity Deployed
How many times you multiply your money. 2× = doubled. SA target: 1.8–2.5× over 10 years.
CGT by Structure
Individual 40% / Trust 80% / Company 80%
Side-by-side CGT comparison. Best structure highlighted. Decide before you sign.
🚨 Critical
Ownership structure decisions are irreversible once the property transfers. Use the CGT comparison before signing the offer to purchase — not after. Consult a tax practitioner.
13Institutional Tab

🏛 Institutional —
underwriting-grade analysis.

The modules a bank credit committee, fund manager, or professional underwriter would run. Four sections:

1 — DSCR Lender Alert

Auto-appears at the top. Red if DSCR < 1.0 (bank won't finance), amber if 1.0–1.2 (marginal), green if ≥1.2 (bankable). Red alert also shows exactly how much to increase the deposit to fix it.

2 — Professional Vitals Dashboard

DSCR
≥1.35× Approved · <1.0× Unbankable
LTV
≤80% Strong · >90% High Risk
OpEx Ratio
<35% Efficient · >50% High OpEx
Break-Even Occupancy
<75% Resilient · >90% Fragile

3 — 10-Year DCF Model

Year-by-year table: Gross Rent, Effective Rent, OpEx (with your expense inflation rate), NOI, Debt Service, Pre-Tax CF, Tax Shield, After-Tax CF, and per-year DSCR. Exit year highlighted in gold. Summary cards show Leveraged IRR, NPV, and MoIC.

4 — Cash-on-Cash Sensitivity Matrix

A 7×7 grid crossing interest rate deltas (−3% to +3%) against vacancy rates (2% to 15%). Each cell shows Cash-on-Cash Return, colour-coded from deep green (>10%) to deep red (<−10%). Shows exactly where the deal breaks.

5 — Capital Stack & Waterfall

Horizontal stacked bar showing Senior Debt / LP Equity / GP Equity proportions. Profit waterfall distributes total DCF returns: LP preferred return first, then excess split with GP promote, showing final MoIC for each party.

14AI Verdict Tab

⚡ AI Verdict —
your second opinion.

1
Ensure your API key is saved
The gold banner at the top must show ✓ Key active. If not, paste your key and click SAVE KEY. One-time setup.
2
Click ⚡ GENERATE ANALYSIS
Scroll below the tab panel to the AI Summary section and click the button. The AI streams a four-paragraph deal memo in real time.
3
Read the verdict
Four sections: Yield & Income · Cash Flow · Risk & Resilience · Verdict. The final paragraph gives STRONG BUY / BUY / HOLD / AVOID with the biggest risk, biggest upside, and one action before you proceed.
4
Re-analyse after changing inputs
Click ↺ RE-ANALYSE any time after updating inputs to get a fresh verdict on the updated numbers.

What the ratings mean

STRONG BUY
IRR clears hurdle, positive cash flow, strong DSCR, robust break-even. Numbers stack up across all dimensions.
BUY
Solid with manageable risks. IRR clears hurdle but may have a monthly top-up or slightly tight break-even.
HOLD
Capital growth may work but yield is weak, DSCR marginal, or risk matrix shows significant downside. Renegotiate.
AVOID
IRR below hurdle, DSCR below 1.0, or deal fails under mild stress. Walk away or restructure fundamentally.
⚠ Important
The AI verdict is not licensed financial advice. It is an automated synthesis of your inputs against market benchmarks. Always verify with a registered financial adviser, bond originator, and tax practitioner before investing.

18FAQ

Common questions
answered directly.

Why doesn't StruclyProp have a submit button?
Every calculation updates live the moment you type. There is nothing to submit — the results panel always reflects your current inputs instantly.
Where is my data stored?
Nowhere external. StruclyProp runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to StruclyProp's servers. If you use the AI Summary, your inputs are sent directly to Anthropic's API under their privacy policy. Your API key is stored only in your browser's sessionStorage and never transmitted to StruclyProp.
Why is my DSCR flagged as a problem?
A DSCR below 1.2× means your NOI is insufficient to meet SA bank financing thresholds. Below 1.0× means rental income alone cannot service the bond at all. Fix by increasing the deposit, reducing the purchase price, or increasing the rent.
Why is IRR showing as — ?
IRR cannot be calculated when cumulative cash flows are negative at all points — the deal never generates a positive return. This happens when exit proceeds are smaller than total equity deployed plus cumulative losses. Try a longer hold period or lower exit cap rate.
How accurate is the transfer duty calculation?
StruclyProp uses the SARS 2024/25 sliding scale: 0% up to R1.1M · 3% on R1.1M–R1.512M · 6% on R1.512M–R2.117M · 8% on R2.117M–R2.723M · 11% above R2.723M. Transfer duty is zero on new builds from a VAT-registered developer. Confirm with your conveyancing attorney before transfer.
Can I use StruclyProp for international properties?
Yes. All inputs are fully adjustable — interest rate, tax rate, acquisition costs, and local fees. The ZAR/USD toggle converts display values. The SARS-specific features (transfer duty, Section 13sex, SARS brackets) apply to SA only. For other markets, enter your local equivalents.
Is the AI verdict reliable enough to act on?
Use it as a structured second opinion, not a final decision. The AI verdict is instructed to write like a bank credit analyst using your actual numbers against SA market benchmarks. It surfaces risks you might not have quantified. It is not a substitute for advice from a registered financial adviser, bond originator, or tax practitioner.
What does the API key cost?
Each AI analysis costs approximately $0.01 (about R0.18) in Anthropic API credits. New accounts receive free credits that cover hundreds of analyses. After that, you load credit in your Anthropic account — there is no subscription, only pay-per-use.
