Not Financial Advice

StruclyProp is a financial modelling and educational tool designed to assist with property investment analysis. It does not constitute financial advice, investment advice, tax advice, legal advice, or any other regulated advice under South African law or the laws of any other jurisdiction.

All outputs — including yields, cash flow projections, capital growth tables, and AI-generated analyst summaries — are illustrative estimates based on the inputs you provide. They are not predictions, guarantees, or recommendations to buy, hold, or sell any property or investment.

AI-Generated Content

The "Analyst Deal Summary" feature uses artificial intelligence to generate deal assessments. This content is automated, not produced by a licensed financial adviser, and may contain errors, omissions, or inaccuracies. The STRONG BUY / BUY / HOLD / AVOID ratings are illustrative outputs only and must not be relied upon as investment recommendations.

Always verify AI-generated content independently before making any financial decision.

Accuracy of Projections

Property markets are unpredictable. Capital growth rates, rental escalation, vacancy rates, interest rates, and operating costs used in StruclyProp are assumptions you supply — actual outcomes will differ. Past South African property market performance does not guarantee future results.

Transfer duty calculations are based on the SARS 2024/25 sliding scale. Tax-related outputs are illustrative — consult a registered tax practitioner for advice specific to your situation.

Professional Advice

Before making any property investment decision, you should consult a registered financial adviser, bond originator, tax practitioner, and attorney. StruclyProp is a tool to inform your due diligence — not to replace it.

Data & Privacy

StruclyProp runs entirely in your browser. No data you enter is transmitted to or stored by the StruclyProp creator. If you use the AI Summary feature, your inputs are sent directly to Anthropic's API using your own API key under Anthropic's privacy policy.
